		
****Basic Bechamel Sauce****
		
		Ingredients
		5 tablespoons unsalted butter  
		1 small yellow onion, diced small
		2 garlic cloves, chopped
		1/3 cup all-purpose flour  
		Coarse salt
		4 1/2 cups whole milk


	Directions
	
		In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium. Add onion and garlic; 
		cook until onion is soft, 4 minutes. Add flour, season with salt, and cook,
		stirring, until mixture is pale golden with a nutty aroma, 4 minutes. 
		Whisking constantly, add half the milk. 
		Add the remaining milk and whisk until smooth. 
		Cook, whisking constantly, until sauce comes to a boil and thickens, 10 minutes.
		Use immediately.
