
****Louise's OatmeaL****
       READY IN: 16mins
       SERVES: 4

      INGREDIENTS
      1 cup old-fashioned oatmeal
      2 1/2 cups water
      1 pinch salt
      1 medium apple, coarsely grated
      1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
      1/3 cup milk
      1/2 teaspoon butter

      DIRECTIONS
      Combine the oatmeal, water and salt in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil.
      Add the grated apple and lemon juice and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
      Add the milk and butter. Stir well and cook for 1 minute.
	  Serve immediately, perhaps with a sprinkle of brown sugar or a drizzle of maple syrup.
