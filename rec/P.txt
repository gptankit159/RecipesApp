
	****Pannakuchen****

	INGREDIENTS
	2/3 cup all-purpose flour
	2/3 cup milk
	1/2 tsp baking powder
	4   eggs
	2   Tbsp butter


	 DIRECTIONS

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
	When preheated, add the butter to a heavy oven safe 10 inch pan (cast iron is ideal) or 9x9 baking dish.
	Place in oven and heat while makeing the batter
	Batter: Put milk, flour, baking powder and eggs in a blender and blend until smooth.
	Can use a submersion blender too.
	Carefully take the pan out of the oven and pour batter on top of melted butter.
	Bake 15-18 minutes
	serve with fresh fruit, scoop of ice cream, or spiced sauted apples and cinnimon ice cream.
	If need be a squirt of lemon juice and a dusting of powdered sugar is fine.
