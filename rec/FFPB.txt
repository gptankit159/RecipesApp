
****French Fried Potato Balls****
	Total Time
		Prep/Total Time: 30 min.

	Makes
		1-1/2 to 2 dozen

	Ingredients
		2 large eggs
		1 cup mashed potatoes
		1/2 cup all-purpose flour
		1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
		1/4 teaspoon salt
		Oil for deep-fat frying
		
	Directions
	In a bowl, beat eggs. Add potatoes. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt;
	stir into potato mixture.
	In a large saucepan or kettle, heat about 1 in. of oil to 375Â°.
	Drop mixture by heaping teaspoonfuls, four or five at a time, into hot oil.
	Fry 2-3 minutes or until golden brown, turning once. Watch closely since they brown quickly.
	Drain on paper towels.
	Serve immediately.
