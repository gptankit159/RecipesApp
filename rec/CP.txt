
****Cabbage and Pasta****
      INGREDIENTS 
      1 large head of cabbage
      2 large onions chopped
      3 clove minced garlic
      1 medium bag of egg noodles
      salt and pepper
      4 Tbsp
      butter
	  
	  DIRECTIONS
      Add Ingredients to Grocery List
      How to Make Cabbage and Pasta
      Saute the onion, garlic and cabbage in butter until tender.
      Add the cooked noodles and season to taste.
      Sprinkle top with paprika
