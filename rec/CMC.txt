
****Creamy Macaroni & Cheese****
       READY IN: 30mins
       SERVES: 4

      INGREDIENTS
       1 egg
       2 cups milk
       2 cups elbow macaroni, cooked & drained
       8 slices American cheese
       1/2 teaspoon salt
       fresh ground pepper
       minced green onion

       DIRECTIONS
         In large sauce pan beat eggs and milk. Stir in macaroni and cheese.
         Cook and stir well over low heat until cheese melts and sauce is thick.
         DO NOT BOIL.
         Stir in salt and pepper.
         Pour in dish and garnish with onions.
